About US

Tannourine spring mineral water is a Lebanese company founded in the year 1978 at Tannourine mountain region, situated at 1500 meters on sea level; our sole activity is filling the natural mineral pure water.

The crystal clear water emerges deep from its underground source and filters through the rock strata into two flow natural gushing springs situated within meters of each others, they tap into the same tanks but in different places.

During the long underground passage, the water acquires its natural balance of a high mineral content while retaining a palatable lightness, making it both fresh and invigorating.

Production Process

  1. Water is drawn from the natural springs through stainless steel pipes directly to the bottling plant.
  2. The water passes through a series of filtration systems to ensure purity and quality.
  3. It is then stored in insulated tanks to maintain its freshness before bottling.
  4. The bottling process is fully automated, ensuring hygiene and efficiency.
  5. Finally, the bottles are sealed, labeled, and packaged for distribution.
