Cedars Drink Tannourine

Production Process

  1. Our water reaches the plant through a food grade 316L stainless steel pipe and does not undergo any treatment whatsoever. We test the raw water frequently for physical, chemical as well as bacteriological parameters as per Libnor standards in our in-house and external lab.
  2. Water is filtered to remove particles and microorganisms present in the raw water if any.
  3. It is then stored in insulated tanks to maintain its freshness before bottling.
  4. The bottling process is fully automated, ensuring hygiene and efficiency.
  5. Finally, the bottles are sealed, labeled, and packaged for distribution.

Our steps follow a stringent quality process:

RAW WATER

  1. Stored in insulated primary tanks.
  2. Physical filtration procedure (bag filter 5 microns, 0.6 micron).
  3. Microbiological filtration procedure (0.45 micron).
  4. Storage of raw water in insulated secondary tanks.

19L GALLON RETURNABLE GALLONS

  1. Pre-inspection, Decapping, Pre-inspection.
  2. Washer comprising of high pressure water rinse.
  3. Acid wash, water rinse, hot detergent wash.
  4. Water rinse, sanitizer wash.
  5. Ozonized water rinse, final rinse.
  6. Filling, cap UV sterilizer and capping.

BOTTLED MINERAL WATER (PET BOTTLES)

  1. Dedusting preforms.
  2. Blowing preform.
  3. Rinsing with cold water.
  4. Filling, cap UV sterilizer and capping.
  5. Date coding.
  6. Sleeve or label application.
